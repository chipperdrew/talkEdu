My overall user rating is incorrect

The overall user rating is calculated from all posts -- even deleted posts. User comments have no effect on the overall user rating.

For example, if you have two posts with 100% rating and five votes each, your user rating should be 1.0. However, if you had deleted a 3rd post with 0% rating and 10 votes, your overall user rating should be 0.5, even though your page will display two posts with 100% rating each.

If you believe your user rating has been inaccurately calculated, please email me.


My post has disappeared

Sometimes the website gets hungry and has to eat posts to stay alive. Rest assured, your post was probably very delicious...

In all seriousness, your post was likely deemed to be spam by the other users of the site. If your post was not spam, it should reappear on the site within a few days. You may still access your post by going to your user page and clicking on the post.
