    My overall user rating is incorrect

    The overall user rating is calculated from all posts -- even deleted posts. For example, if you have 2 posts with 100% rating and 5 votes each, your user rating should be 1.0. However, if you had deleted a 3rd post with 0% rating and 10 votes, your overall user rating should be 0.5, even though your page will display 2 posts with 100% rating each.

    If you believe your user rating has been inaccurately calculated, please email me. {# EMAIL #}


    My post has disappeared

    Sometimes the website gets hungry and has to eat posts to stay alive. Rest assured, your post was probably very delicious...

    In all seriousness, your post was likely deemed to be spam by the other users of the site. If your post was not spam, it should reappear on the site within a few days. You may still access your post by going to your user page and clicking on the post.


    I can't log in

    There are a couple of possibilities:

    1. The incorrect password is being entered.

      2. Please change your password. You may do so by clicking here.

    2. The incorrect username is being entered.

      3. If you have made any posts/comments on the site, try searching for them and finding the username attached to them. If worst comes to worst, you may try emailing me {# EMAIL #}, but the odds are slim that I will be able to help (send me every piece of info you can -- email, posts voted on, last login, etc).

    3. The account has been banned.

      4. If you are sure you are entering in the correct username and password, your account is likely banned. This is probably the result of someone stealing your password and posting as you. Depending on my mood, I may return your account to you. Press your luck by emailing me here {# EMAIL #}.


    Can I donate to you?

    Of course, but I would suggest against it. The purpose of this site is to stimulate discussion about US education, so naturally your time and resources would be better spent on making education better in this country.

    If, for whatever reason, you still feel inclined to donate, you may do so by clicking here {# DONATE #}.


    I'm locked out of my account

    Sorry! For security reasons, the number of logins you can attempt before being locked out of your account is limited. Please email me {# EMAIL #} and I will unlock your account.
